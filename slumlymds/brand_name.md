Here are some fun, catchy name ideas for your slumbook-style social media platform:
Nostalgic / Classic Feel

Slumly – simple, modern take on "slumbook"
PassNote – captures the note-passing essence
FoldedNotes – evokes passing folded paper in class
NotePass – clean and descriptive

Playful / Trendy

Notesy – cute and memorable
Passlum – blend of "pass" + "slumbook"
Scribbl – playful, like writing notes
DearPage – personal, diary-like feel

Community / Connection Focused

PageMate – "page" + "classmate" vibes
Penpal Circle – friendly, community feel
NoteChain – notes passed from person to person
Ripple Notes – notes spreading through friends

Short & Brandable

Slumly ⭐ (top pick — short, recognizable, easy to say)
Notevo – note + evolution
Passlr – trendy app-style name
Quilly – from "quill," writing instrument


Top Recommendation: Slumly.com — it instantly signals the slumbook concept, feels modern, and works great as an app name or brand. Runner-up would be PassNote for its clarity.
